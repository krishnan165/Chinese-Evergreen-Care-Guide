Chinese Evergreen Care Guide

Soil for Chinese Evergreen

Well-draining, peat-based soil mix.

Chinese Evergreen thrives in well-draining soil with a peat-based mix, providing good moisture retention.

Watering

Moderate watering, allowing the top inch of soil to dry between waterings.

Water the plant moderately, allowing the soil surface to dry before the next watering to prevent overwatering.

Lighting

Low to bright, indirect light.

Place the Chinese Evergreen in a location with low to bright, indirect light; avoid direct sunlight.

Benefits

Adaptable to various light conditions.

Chinese Evergreen can thrive in a wide range of light conditions, making it suitable for different indoor spaces.

Air-purifying qualities.

This plant helps improve indoor air quality by filtering out common pollutants.

Non-Benefits

May be sensitive to overwatering; avoid waterlogged soil.
